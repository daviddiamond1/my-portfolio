A light weight way to engage your mobile users

Push messages are a new message channel for Intercom. They allow customers with our mobile SDKs installed to send their users a push notification that points to a specific place in their app, directly from Intercom.

Harnessing the power of Intercom for push messages

Send push messages automatically based on user events and personalise them using custom data. Intercom users can even start a campaign from their push messages which drive people towards a common goal by sending the perfect series of push messages.

Design details

  • Know exactly how your messages will look before sending them

    1

    The composer is designed to show you exactly how your messages will look when your user views them on their device. To give the best experience possible, we designed preview states for each platform. The composer can also be put into preview mode which will truncate the message content to show you what your user will first see when they get your push.

  • Personalise your push messages

    2

    Make your push messages relevant and personal by using custom data like users names and any other data you store in Intercom within push notifications.

  • Send your push messages manually or automatically

    3

    You can choose to send your messages manually or automatically. This means that when a user takes an action within your app you can trigger a push message to be sent to them straight away.

  • Target the right users

    4

    Only send your message to the right users by using Intercom's powerful filtering options.

  • Measure how your push message perform

    5

    View the amount of users engaged with your message and even see who engaged with your message to target them later.
