Intercom Push Messaging

Push messages are a new message channel for Intercom. They allow customers with our mobile SDKs installed to send their users a push notification that points to a specific place in their app, directly from Intercom. Send push messages automatically based on user events and personalise them using custom data. Intercom users can even start a campaign from their push messages which drive people towards a common goal by sending the perfect series of push messages.
