Pottery Offices

Kouchin came to me to build a site that showcased their group of furnished workspaces and allowed people to check the availability of them and get an estimate of how much it would cost to rent them. The site is built on Jekyll using Tachyons as a CSS framework, the booking system is a custom script built with the help of Dave Mackintosh that allows Kouchin to manage their bookings from a simple spreadsheet. The project was pretty hands on, I worked on everything from the design, development and even photography. We had a tight deadline but managed to ship the project in just 3 weeks.
